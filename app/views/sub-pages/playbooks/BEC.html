Disclaimer

If you are currently experiencing a ransomware incident, you should immediately activate your cyber response plan. This will give you specific actions that need to be taken to restore your system.

All settings must now have a 3-2-1 backup process in place which should now be applied. You should contact your IT provider for support or reach out to the RPA if you are a member.

If your incident plan isn’t available or you don’t have a cyber response process, it's crucial to use and adapt the steps in this playbook.

Tailoring the actions to your setting will give you the best possible chance to limit impact and begin recovery to daily operations.
