The RPA provides comprehensive risk protection for public sector schools and is an alternative to commercial insurance for the education sector. Here are some key points:


Coverage: The service is funded by the UK government and provides coverage for losses due to unforeseen events, including cyber attacks.

Membership: Public sector schools can join the RPA, which offers a range of benefits including access to expert support during incidents.

Conditions: To be eligible for cyber cover, schools must meet specific conditions such as having offline backups, completing NCSC cyber security training, registering with Police CyberAlarm, and having a Cyber Response Plan.

Benefits: Joining the RPA provides schools with a vital safety net, ensuring they have the support needed to recover from cyber incidents. Expert help is available 24/7 with RPA cover, just a phone call away should an incident occur.
