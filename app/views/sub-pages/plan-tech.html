Plan technology for your school is a free support service designed to help all schools in England effectively plan and benchmark their school’s technology against the DfE’s Digital and Technology Standards.  

The service can support your school in developing a robust digital strategy and provides bespoke recommendations to help you meet the standards. It can also help to improve online safety as well as support in increasing resilience against cyber incidents.  

The service is available to schools in England and anyone with a DfE Sign-in linked to your school can gain access.
